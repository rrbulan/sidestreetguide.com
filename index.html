Weather

Bring layers.

Visitors often pack for Southern California. SF has other ideas, especially near the water.

Traffic

Distance ≠ time.

Four and a half miles can take twenty minutes or two hours depending on games, concerts, cruise ships, and traffic.

Experience

Ride a Waymo.

It may be transportation, but for many visitors it becomes one of the most memorable parts of the trip.

Street Smarts

Skip the shell game.

If spectators seem to be winning money on the corner, some may be part of the act.
